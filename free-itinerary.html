Local Tips & Essential Information

More details in Premium

Travel Basics

Weather

May in Barcelona: 59-72°F (15-22°C), mostly sunny with occasional showers

Currency

Euro (€). Credit cards widely accepted. ATMs readily available.

Language

Spanish and Catalan. English is commonly spoken in tourist areas.

Transportation

Metro, bus, and taxis are convenient. Consider T-Casual card (10 journeys).

Local Tips

Dining Schedule

Lunch: 1-3pm, Dinner: 8-11pm. Many restaurants close between meals.

Safety

Watch for pickpockets on Las Ramblas and in busy tourist areas.

Tipping

Not mandatory but 5-10% is appreciated for good service.

Must-Try Food

Paella, tapas, pan con tomate, crema catalana, and local cava (sparkling wine).
